Bio

Hello! and welcome to my website!
Shiv's journey into tech started at the end of 2021 when he decided to self study web development, in May of 2022 he decided to commit full time to his goal by enrolling in classes to polish his new skills as a developer. He is currently a Software Engineering student at General Assembly in the Software Engineering Immersive Program. Upon graduation in August 2022 he hopes to join a team that challenges and supports him to be his best as a Junior Developer, he brings experience from a wide range of industries including real estate development, insurance, hospitality, and construction. Shiv resides in Denver, CO but is originally from Baltimore, MD. Shiv is passionate about learning new things, especially about topics related to Science, Engineering, Construction, Philosophy, Spirituality, or Technology. He enjoys to build things ranging from small construction projects around the home, to small film reels with his friend Ian, a talented digital media content creator, and obviously web development projects. During his free time he likes to snowboard, attend concerts, hike, and camp.
